BMW X5

Luxury Meets Power

The BMW X5 combines rugged SUV performance with the luxury and innovation you expect from BMW. Featuring intelligent all-wheel drive, adaptive suspension, and powerful engine options, the X5 is built to conquer any road with style.

  • Engine: 3.0L BMW TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder
  • Horsepower: 335 hp
  • 0-100 km/h: 5.3 seconds
  • Drive: xDrive AWD
  • Technology: iDrive 8.0, 12.3” Display, BMW Intelligent Personal Assistant
